What is Campus Meet?

Campus Meet is a zonal-level initiative organized by the Jesus Youth Changanacherry Campus Ministry, designed to help campus students deepen their relationship with Christ. This program provides a platform for spiritual growth and community building, empowering students to strengthen their faith and live out their Christian values.

THEME

#connect

Form relationships that reflect the unity found in Christ.

#grow

Deepen your spiritual life to align with Christ's teachings.

#build

Actively contribute to establishing Christ's Kingdom on earth.
