What is Campus Meet?

Campus Meet is a zonal-level initiative by the Jesus Youth Changanacherry Campus Ministry, tailored for first and second year students who are new to Jesus Youth. This program aims to deepen their relationship with Christ, offering a platform for spiritual growth, community building, and empowering them to live out their Christian values.
